Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

IndieCalc ("we," "us," or "our") operates the indiecalc.com website (the "Site"). This page describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Calculator inputs. When you use our calculators, you enter financial information such as income, filing status, and state of residence. This data is processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your calculator inputs.

Automatically collected data. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Cloudflare Web Analytics is a privacy-first analytics service that does not use cookies or collect personal data. It collects:

  • Page views and visits
  • Referring URL
  • Country (derived from Cloudflare’s edge network, not your IP)
  • Browser and device type

Your IP address is not stored or logged by our analytics.

Email. If you use our "Email Me This Report" feature, it opens your default email client with a pre-filled message. We do not capture or store your email address through this feature.

2. Cookies

IndieCalc does not set any first-party cookies. Cloudflare Web Analytics is cookie-free. If we add third-party services in the future that use cookies, we will update this policy accordingly.

3. How We Use Information

We use analytics data to:

  • Understand which calculators and states are most popular
  • Identify and fix bugs or errors in calculations
  • Improve the Site's content and user experience

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

4. Third-Party Services

The Site uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: Hosting, CDN, and analytics. See Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Web fonts. See Google’s Privacy Policy.

We may include affiliate links to third-party services. Clicking these links may result in those third parties collecting data subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Choices

Since we do not use cookies or collect personal data, there are no tracking preferences to manage. You can use the Site freely without any data being tied to your identity.

6. Data Retention

We do not store your calculator inputs. Cloudflare Web Analytics data is retained and aggregated by Cloudflare according to their standard retention policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact@indiecalc.com
